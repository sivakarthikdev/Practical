Teachable Moment

January is National Soup Month! In 2010, scientists found a 2,400-year-old soup pot in China. The pot was tightly closed. When the scientists opened it, they found liquid soup. There were also bones in the liquid.

Fun Fact

Washing your hands kills germs that can make you sick. You should wash them after using the bathroom and before eating. Use warm water and enough soap to make bubbles. Scrub the backs of your hands, between your fingers, and around your nails. Wash your hands for about 20 seconds. You should use a clean towel to dry your hands.
